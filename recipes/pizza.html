Margherita Pizza Recipe

Description

Originally made in Naples, Italy in 1889 for Queen Margherita of Savoy, the Margherita Pizza has grown to become a staple of Italian style pizza. However, one does not need to travel to Italy to taste the classic dish. The iconic Margherita pizza is a delicious yet simple to make dish that, with this recipe guide, you'll be able to make at home with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough (Homemade OR Store-bought)
  • Mozarella
  • Finely Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano OR Salt
  • San Marzano Tomato Sauce OR any Tomato Sauce with Ripe Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Extra- Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to it's highest temperature for at least 30 minutes
  2. Place Pizza Dough on lightly floured surface
  3. Form Pizza Dough into circle
    4. If using San Marzano Tomato Sauce: Pour sauce into bowl and crush tomatoes
  4. Spread Tomato Sauce evenly on dough. Make sure to leave space for crust to form
  5. Place Mozarella on Tomato Sauce
  6. Place pizza on a pan or cooking tray
  7. Place pan or cooking tray in preheated oven
  8. Cook for 7 to 8 minutes
  9. Safely take out pizza
  10. Place a few pieces of Basil on top
  11. Drizzle some Extra- Virgin Olive Oil on top
  12. Sprinkle some Parmigiano-Reggiano OR Salt on top
  13. Slice pizza and ENJOY!
