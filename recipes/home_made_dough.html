Home Made Dough

Description

Store-bought pizza dough is convenient and readily-available, but usually costs more, and gives the customer no control over the ingredients the manufacturer's used to make it. Making your own pizza dough might sound daunting at first, but with this recipe guide, you'll be able to easily create a dough that will take your pizza to the next level. With just a few ingredients, you'll have quality pizza dough that can be sliced into multiple dough balls and saved for anytime you crave pizza.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups of All-Purpose Flour OR Bread Flour (Bread Flour will result in a better crust)
  • 1 Teaspoon of Sugar
  • 1 Standard Packet of Yeast (2 1/4 Teaspoons)
  • 2 Teaspoons of Salt
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Water at 110°F OR 43°C
  • 2 Tablespoons and 2 Teaspoons of Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Grab 2 bowls (1 for Mixing and 1 for Resting)
  2. Pour 2 Teaspoons of Olive Oil in your Resting bowl
  3. Pour all ingredients in your Mixing Bowl
  4. Mix (by hand or with a mixer) until ingredients combine
  5. Remove dough from bowl and place into lightly floured surface
  6. Gently knead dough into ball for 5 minutes
    • If dough is sticky, add flour (1 Tablespoon at a time) and knead
    • If dough is dry, add water (1 Tablespoon at a time) and knead
  7. Grab Resting Bowl and spread the Olive Oil inside
  8. Place dough ball in Resting Bowl
  9. Cover bowl with plastic wrap or towel
  10. Let dough rest and grow in warm, dry area for 1 hour (Overnight would result in a better dough)
  11. Take dough out and divide it into desired size
  12. Refrigirate leftovers
